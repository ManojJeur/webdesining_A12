Overview

Khelo India is an initiative by the Government of India to promote sports and physical fitness among young people.

History and Background

KheloIndia was launched in 2018 to revive the sports culture in India and create a sustainable ecosystem for nurturing sporting talent.

Mission and Vision

Our mission is to create a sporting culture in India that promotes active living, teamwork, and sportsmanship. Our vision is to make India a global sports powerhouse by nurturing young talent and providing them with world-class facilities and training.
