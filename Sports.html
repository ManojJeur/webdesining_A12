Sports in India

India is home to a diverse range of sports, from traditional games like kabaddi and kho-kho to modern sports like cricket, football, and hockey. The Khelo India initiative aims to promote all of these sports and more, providing resources and support for aspiring athletes across the country.

Popular Sports in India

Cricket

Cricket is by far the most popular sport in India, with millions of fans and players across the country. The Indian national team is one of the strongest in the world, and domestic leagues like the Indian Premier League (IPL) attract top talent from around the globe.

Football

Football is also growing in popularity in India, with the Indian Super League (ISL) attracting large crowds and TV audiences. The national team has made significant progress in recent years, and there is a renewed focus on developing young talent through grassroots programs.
